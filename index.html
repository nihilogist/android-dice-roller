A dice roller for RPGs

The RPG Dice Roller app will contain a dice roller for several popular RPG systems, to help speed up play by taking out some of the dice-sorting and maths that can slow things down.

Project status

The first version of the app is in open beta testing on the Play Store. For access to the latest Beta version, you can find it at: the Play Store.

The planned milestones for releases can be found on the milestones page

RPG systems supported

Currently the following systems are supported

  • 7th Sea (first edition only)
